Pat Bandoni

I was born in Fresno Ca. Drawing and painting have always been a part of my life. My favorite mediums are oils and pastels. My style is changeable. Painting is a gift that I give myself. It's my time to create and play. Flowers are one of my favorite subjects. I taught floral design classes for Fresno Adult School and worked for my favorite florist at Flowers by Marie. The collection here covers a wide spectrum of styles, with different palettes and color schemes.
